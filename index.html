Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my jewelry? +

To keep your jewelry looking its best, we recommend:

  • Storing pieces separately to prevent scratching
  • Avoiding contact with chemicals, perfumes, and lotions
  • Cleaning with a soft cloth and mild soap if needed
  • Removing jewelry before swimming or showering
What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn jewelry in its original condition with tags and packaging. Custom pieces are final sale unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Do you offer custom designs? +

Yes! We love creating custom pieces. Please contact us with your ideas, and our designers will work with you to create a unique piece that meets your vision and budget.

Are your gemstones ethically sourced? +

Absolutely. We are committed to ethical sourcing and only work with suppliers who adhere to responsible mining practices and fair labor standards.
